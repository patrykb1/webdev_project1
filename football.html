Legia Warsaw

Legia Warsaw is a football team that plays in Warsaw, Poland

Stadium

The team plays at the Polish Army Stadium, which was also known as the Pepsi Arena between 2011-2014. The stadium has a capacity of 31,103 and costed €110,000,000 to build

Trophies

  • Ekstraklasa (Polish League) - 15 times
  • Puchar Polski (Polish Cup) - 19 times
  • Superpuchar Polski (Polish Supercup) - 4 times

Ultras

Legia Warsaw has a large and passionate fanbase, known as the "Zyletas" (which translates to "the Razors"). They are known for their vocal support during matches and their impressive TIFOs that send powerful messages
